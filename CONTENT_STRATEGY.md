## Microcopy
- Hover states: "Learn more", "View case study", "Get in touch"
- Error states: clear, helpful errors (e.g., "Please enter a valid email address")
- Loading states: "Sending..." on form submit; "Loading projects..." for grid

## Trust Signals
- Mention of tech stack prominently
- Accessibility badge or mention (WCAG AA)
- Performance metrics (like "We achieve Lighthouse >90")
- Testimonials with real names (if available) or plausible titles
- Possibly mention years of experience or number of projects (estimate if unknown)

## CTA Language
- Primary: "Get Started", "Start a Project", "Contact Us", "Schedule a Call"
- Secondary: "View Our Work", "Learn More", "See Services"
- Keep consistent: one primary CTA per section, secondary CTA less prominent.
