# SITEMAP.md - Site Structure & Navigation

## Pages

1. **/** - Home
   - Hero section (main CTA)
   - Services preview (3-4 highlights with "View All" link to /services)
   - Why PARADOX (features/benefits grid)
   - Testimonials
   - CTA section (contact/schedule call)
   - Footer

2. **/about** - About
   - Company story
   - Mission & values
   - Leadership team (CEO + key leads)
   - Milestones or timeline
   - CTA to contact

3. **/services** - Services
   - Overview intro
   - Service cards (each with icon, title, description, maybe link to detail)
     - Custom Frontend Development
     - Performance Optimization
     - Accessibility Audits & Remediation
     - AI Integration & Automation
     - Design Systems & Component Libraries
   - Process section (Discovery → Design → Build → Deploy → Support)
   - Case study highlights (2-3 snippets)
   - CTA to discuss project

4. **/work** - Portfolio
   - Intro paragraph
   - Project grid (6-8 items)
   - Each card: image, title, description, tech tags, link to case study
   - Optional filter by service type
   - CTA to see more or contact

5. **/contact** - Contact
   - Contact form (name, email, company, message)
   - Contact details (email, phone)
   - Map placeholder
   - Social links
   - FAQ accordion (optional)

## Header Navigation

- Logo (text: PARADOX, with maybe subtle mark)
- Links: Home, About, Services, Work, Contact
- CTA button: "Get Started" (links to /contact)

## Footer Navigation

- About PARADOX (short blurb + logo)
- Quick Links: Home, About, Services, Work, Contact
- Contact Info: email, phone
- Social icons: LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub
- Copyright and legal links (Privacy Policy placeholder, Terms placeholder)

## CTA Flow

- Primary CTA: "Get Started" → Contact page form
- Secondary CTA: "View Our Work" → /work
- Tertiary: "Learn More" about services → /services
- In-content: consistent primary buttons for next steps

## URL Policy

- Clean URLs (no .html)
- Lowercase with hyphens
- Avoid deep nesting; all pages at root level
